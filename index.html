Interactive Robot Art Installation

My name is Mischa and I create interactive artworks using robots to draw on a canvas while driving, controlled by both humans and AI in a creative collaboration.

Find Out More

What is the Interactive Robot Art Installation about?

How can humans interact with robots and AI to create together an artpiece? How do humans behave when they have the control over robots? How will the outcome of one thousend interactions look like on a canvas? How will society look like when robots are always and everywhere around us?

These are only some questions that I try to address with my installation. It's about innovative technologies and about our future as humans.

And I also hope to inspire participants of my installation to embrace the future technologies instead of being afraid of it.
